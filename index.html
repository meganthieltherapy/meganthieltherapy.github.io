Compassionate Care for Your Mental Health Journey

Everyone deserves affordable, accessible mental health services. Whether you're navigating grief, working through trauma, managing anxiety or depression, or facing major life transitions, I'm here to support you every step of the way.

My approach is centered on creating a safe, non-judgmental space where you can explore your feelings, develop coping strategies, and work toward healing and growth.

Services Offered

1. Telehealth Services

I accept some insurance and offer a sliding fee scale. Everyone deserves affordable services, and I'm committed to making therapy accessible to you regardless of your financial situation.

2. Support Groups

Join others on similar journeys in a supportive group environment. Support groups are available for $10-15 per hour, providing an affordable way to connect and heal with others.

3. Walk and Talk Services

Experience therapy in a natural, outdoor setting. Walk and talk sessions combine the benefits of movement and nature with therapeutic conversation for a unique healing experience.

4. Veterinarian Grief Training

Specialized training for veterinary professionals on how to compassionately support clients through pet loss and grief. Equipping veterinarians with the tools and skills to navigate these difficult conversations with empathy and understanding.
